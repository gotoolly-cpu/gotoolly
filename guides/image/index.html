Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compress images for the web without losing quality?

Use the Image Compressor tool. It balances file size reduction with visual quality. Start with 80% quality for photos and adjust up or down based on your needs. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Can I remove the background from an image for free?

Yes. The Background Removal tool lets you remove backgrounds from images and save as transparent PNG. It works entirely in your browser with no uploads to any server.

What image formats can I convert between?

You can convert between JPG, PNG, WebP, AVIF, and SVG formats. Each converter includes quality settings and format-specific options like background fill for transparency.

How do I upscale an image without losing quality?

Use the Image Upscaler tool. It uses browser-based interpolation to enlarge images while minimizing quality loss. You can choose from different upscaling algorithms and target sizes.

Can I batch convert multiple images at once?

Yes. The Batch Image Converter lets you select multiple images and convert them between formats simultaneously. All processing happens locally in your browser.
