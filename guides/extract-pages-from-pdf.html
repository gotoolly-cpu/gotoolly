Professional Tips for PDF Extraction

💡 Tip: For large PDFs, use "Select All" first, then uncheck unwanted pages. This is much faster than checking many individual boxes.

💡 Tip: Extracted pages maintain their original quality. Images, fonts, and formatting remain exactly as in the source PDF.

💡 Tip: Need non-consecutive pages? Simply check each page you need�they'll appear in the extracted PDF in the same order as the original document.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I reorder pages when extracting?

Our tool maintains the original page order from your source PDF. If you need to reorder pages, consider using a PDF editor after extraction or check our future tools for page reordering functionality.

What happens to formatting and quality?

All formatting, images, fonts, and quality are preserved exactly as in the original PDF. The extraction process creates a perfect copy of the selected pages.

Is my PDF secure during extraction?

Yes! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your PDF file is never uploaded to any server. We cannot access, view, or store your documents.

Can I extract from password-protected PDFs?

For security reasons, password-protected PDFs cannot be processed. Please remove the password protection first using appropriate software, then use our tool for extraction.

What's the maximum file size?

PDFs up to 25MB can be processed efficiently. Larger files may require more processing time depending on your device's capabilities.

Can I extract pages from scanned PDFs?

Yes! Our tool works with both text-based and image-based (scanned) PDFs. However, scanned PDFs will remain as images in the extracted document.
