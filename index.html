Why Choose GoToolly Over Other Online Tools?

Many online tools upload files because server-side processing is easier to scale, easier to control, and easier to monetize. GoToolly makes a deliberate, privacy‑first choice: we run tools in your browser so your files stay on your device. That means some server‑only features are intentionally unavailable — a trade‑off we accept for keeping your data private.

Privacy by design

No uploads — your files are processed locally in the browser. We cannot access or store your documents, images, or text.

Honest trade‑offs

Some tasks (advanced OCR, massive batch conversions) require server compute and are not offered here. When server processing is necessary, we link to appropriate alternatives and explain the trade‑offs.

Fast & predictable

Processing completes as soon as your device finishes the task, so results are immediate and predictable — limited by your device, not by upload queues.

Frequently asked questions

How do browser‑based tools work?

They use standard browser features — JavaScript, Canvas, Web Workers, and WebAssembly where applicable — to process files locally on your device. No file upload is required to use the core functions.

Are there file size or feature limits?

Yes. Limits depend on your device and browser memory. Most tools handle files up to ~100MB; very large files, extensive batch jobs, or compute‑heavy tasks may not work reliably in the browser.

When should I NOT use GoToolly?

Don’t use GoToolly when you need server‑grade processing: high‑accuracy OCR on scanned images, enterprise‑scale batch conversions, video/transcoding, or DRM‑protected files. In those cases we link to recommended server‑based alternatives and explain the trade‑offs.

Is anything uploaded or stored?

No — core tools do not upload or store your files. We do use privacy-respecting, minimal analytics to improve the site, but we do not collect or retain the files you process with the tools.

Use privacy‑first tools that keep files on your device

Most tools are free to use and supported by unobtrusive advertising. If a task needs server processing, we will tell you and recommend alternatives — no surprises.
