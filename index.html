Why GoToolly?

Privacy-first by design, not by marketing

Many online tools upload files because server-side processing is easier to scale. GoToolly makes a deliberate choice: we run tools in your browser so your files stay on your device.

Privacy by design

No uploads — your files are processed locally in the browser. We cannot access or store your documents, images, or text.

Honest trade-offs

Some tasks require server compute and are not offered here. When server processing is needed, we link to alternatives and explain the trade-offs.

Fast & predictable

Results are immediate — limited by your device, not by upload queues or server load.
