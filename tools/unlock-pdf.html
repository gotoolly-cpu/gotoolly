Frequently Asked Questions

Is unlocking a PDF free?
Yes, unlocking a PDF is completely free. No registration or payment required.
Can I unlock any password-protected PDF?
You can unlock PDFs if you know the correct password. Some PDFs use advanced encryption like AES-256 that may not be supported in browser-based tools.
Is it legal to unlock a PDF?
You should only unlock PDF files that you own or have explicit authorization to modify. Unlocking PDFs without permission may violate copyright laws.
Does my file leave my computer?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your file never leaves your device.
