Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a TAR file?

Select one or more files, then click 'Download TAR' to create and download a TAR archive. TAR format bundles files without compression.

Does TAR compress files?

No. TAR is an archiving format that bundles files together without compression. To compress, you would typically apply GZIP compression after creating the TAR file.

Is my data uploaded?

No. All TAR creation happens entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
