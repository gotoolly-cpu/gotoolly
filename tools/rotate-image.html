Frequently Asked Questions

What rotation angles are available?

You can rotate by 90° clockwise, 90° counter-clockwise, 180°, or use the custom slider from 0° to 360°.

Does rotation change the image dimensions?

Yes. For 90° and 270° rotations, the width and height swap. The canvas auto-fits to the new dimensions so nothing is cropped.

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WebP images are supported. All processing is done locally in your browser.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All rotation is performed entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
