How It Works

Select the type of percentage calculation you need, enter the values, and get instant results. All calculations are performed entirely in your browser.

  • X% of Y: Multiply Y by X divided by 100. Formula: (X / 100) × Y
  • X is what % of Y: Divide X by Y, then multiply by 100. Formula: (X / Y) × 100
  • Percentage Increase: ((New - Original) / Original) × 100
  • Percentage Decrease: ((Original - New) / Original) × 100
  • Percentage Difference: (|X - Y| / ((X + Y) / 2)) × 100
  • Reverse Percentage: (X / Y) × 100

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between percentage increase and percentage difference?
Percentage increase measures growth from an original value to a new value. Percentage difference measures the relative difference between two values without designating one as the original.
What is reverse percentage?
Reverse percentage finds the original number when you know a part and its percentage. For example, if 50 is 25% of a number, the answer is 200.
