Frequently Asked Questions

What page sizes are available?

A4 (210x297mm) and Letter (8.5x11in) page sizes are supported, in both portrait and landscape orientations.

Can I reorder images before converting?

Yes. You can drag and drop thumbnails to reorder, or use the up/down buttons. The order determines the PDF page order.

What image formats can I use?

PNG, JPG, and WebP images are supported. Each image becomes one page in the PDF.

How are images fitted to the page?

Images are scaled to fit within the page dimensions while respecting the margin settings. Aspect ratio is preserved.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All PDF generation is done entirely in your browser using the pdf-lib library. Your images never leave your device.
