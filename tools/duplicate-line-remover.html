Frequently Asked Questions

How does the duplicate line remover work?

Paste your text, configure options (sort, case sensitivity, trim whitespace), and click Remove Duplicates. The tool keeps only the first occurrence of each line and removes all duplicates.

Can I sort the unique lines alphabetically?

Yes. Check the Sort alphabetically option before clicking Remove Duplicates. The output will be sorted A-Z.

Is my text uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
