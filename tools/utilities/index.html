Frequently Asked Questions

Are these online utilities really free?

Yes, all 23 utilities are completely free to use. No sign-up, no usage limits, no premium tiers. Everything runs in your browser.

Can I generate QR codes and barcodes?

Yes, you can generate QR codes from URLs, text, or contact info, and barcodes in Code 128, Code 39, EAN-13, and UPC-A formats, downloadable as PNG or SVG.

Do you support UUID generation?

Yes, we support both UUID v4 (random) and UUID v7 (time-ordered) generation with bulk options. All generation happens locally in your browser.

Can I decode and generate JWT tokens?

Yes. The JWT Decoder inspects header, payload, and signature locally. The JWT Generator lets you build and sign tokens with HMAC for development.

What calculators and converters are available?

We offer a Percentage Calculator, BMI Calculator, Loan Calculator with amortization, Unit Converter for length/weight/temperature/volume/speed, and a Unix Timestamp Converter.

How these tools work: All processing happens in your browser. Files are not uploaded to servers. Nothing is logged or stored.
