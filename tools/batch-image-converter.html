Frequently Asked Questions

How many images can I convert at once?

You can upload and convert up to 20 images at a time. For larger batches, process in multiple rounds.

What formats are supported?

Input: JPG, PNG, WebP. Output: JPG, PNG, WebP. All processing is done locally in your browser.

Can I download all images at once?

If JSZip is loaded, you can download all converted images as a ZIP file. Otherwise, you can download each image individually.

Does quality affect file size?

Yes. Higher quality produces larger files. For web use, 80-90% quality offers a good balance between file size and visual quality.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All conversions are performed entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
