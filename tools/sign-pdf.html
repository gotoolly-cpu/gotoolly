Frequently Asked Questions

Is signing a PDF free?
Yes, signing a PDF is completely free.
What signature methods are available?
You can draw a signature using your mouse/touch, type your name in a cursive font, or upload an image of your signature.
Is this a legally binding digital signature?
No, this creates a visual signature image on the PDF. It is not a cryptographic digital signature and may not be legally binding in all jurisdictions.
Does my file leave my computer?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser.
