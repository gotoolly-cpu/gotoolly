Frequently Asked Questions

Are these SEO tools really free?

Yes. All 20 SEO tools are completely free with no sign-up required. Every tool runs directly in your browser with no hidden costs, usage limits, or premium tiers.

Is my website data sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your URLs, meta tags, HTML content, and any other data never leave your device. Nothing is uploaded, stored, or logged on any server.

How does the Schema Markup Generator work?

Fill in the fields for your content type (article, product, FAQ, breadcrumb, or organization) and the tool generates valid JSON-LD structured data. Copy the output and paste it into your page's HTML head section or use Google Tag Manager to deploy it.

Can I use the SERP Preview to test my pages?

Yes. The SERP Preview tool shows you exactly how your page title and meta description will appear in Google search results. You can adjust character counts to ensure your titles stay under 60 characters and descriptions stay under 160 characters for optimal display.

What is the difference between the Meta Tag Generator and Meta Tag Analyzer?

The Meta Tag Generator creates complete meta tag code based on your page details, including title, description, keywords, and social tags. The Meta Tag Analyzer evaluates your existing meta tags and provides recommendations for improvement based on current SEO best practices.

How these tools work: All processing happens in your browser. Your URLs, meta tags, and SEO data are never uploaded to any server. Nothing is logged or stored.
