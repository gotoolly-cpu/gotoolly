Frequently Asked Questions

Are these PDF tools really free?

Yes, all 25 PDF tools are completely free to use. There are no hidden charges, usage limits, or premium tiers. Every tool works entirely in your browser.

Do you upload my PDF to a server?

No. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I convert Excel or PowerPoint to PDF?

Yes, you can convert Excel spreadsheets and PowerPoint presentations to PDF format. The conversion happens entirely in your browser with no file uploads.

How do I unlock a protected PDF?

Use the Unlock PDF tool. You will need to enter the document password to remove protection. All processing is done locally in your browser.

Can I add page numbers or watermarks to my PDF?

Yes, the PDF Page Numbers tool lets you add auto-numbering with multiple format and position options. The PDF Watermark tool lets you add text watermarks with custom opacity, rotation, and color.

How these tools work: All processing happens in your browser. Files are not uploaded to servers. Nothing is logged or stored.
