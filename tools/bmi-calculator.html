How It Works

Enter your height and weight to calculate your Body Mass Index. The tool categorizes your result and shows your healthy weight range.

  • Metric: Enter height in centimeters and weight in kilograms. BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)²
  • Imperial: Enter height in feet and inches and weight in pounds. BMI = (weight / height²) × 703
  • Categories: Underweight (<18.5), Normal (18.5-24.9), Overweight (25-29.9), Obese (30+)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a healthy BMI?
A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy for most adults. Below 18.5 is underweight, 25-29.9 is overweight, and 30+ is obese.
Is BMI accurate for athletes?
BMI may overestimate body fat in athletes and those with high muscle mass. It does not distinguish between muscle and fat.
Is this tool a substitute for medical advice?
No. This is for informational purposes only. Always consult a healthcare professional for health-related decisions.
