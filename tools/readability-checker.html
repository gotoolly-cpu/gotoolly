Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Flesch-Kincaid grade level?

The Flesch-Kincaid grade level estimates the U.S. school grade level needed to understand the text. A score of 8 means an 8th grader can understand it.

What is a good reading ease score?

A score between 60-70 is considered standard and easy to read. Scores above 70 are fairly easy, while scores below 50 are difficult to read.

Is my text uploaded to a server?

No. All analysis happens entirely in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
