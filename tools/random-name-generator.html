How It Works

1 Choose Options

Select the gender, country, and number of names you want to generate.

2 Generate

Click Generate and the tool randomly combines first and last names from our curated database.

3 Export

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the random name generator work?

The tool randomly combines first names and last names from our curated database of names from multiple countries and genders to generate realistic-sounding names.

Can I generate names for a specific country?

Yes. You can select from US, UK, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Japan, India, Brazil, and Russia. Each country has its own curated name database.

What genders are supported?

You can generate male, female, neutral names, or choose 'All' to get a mix of all genders.

Is my data uploaded to a server?

No. All name generation happens entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
