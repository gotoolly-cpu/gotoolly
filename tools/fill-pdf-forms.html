Frequently Asked Questions

Is filling PDF forms free?
Yes, filling PDF forms is completely free.
What types of PDF forms are supported?
This tool supports AcroForm (standard PDF form) fields. XFA forms (LiveCycle) are not supported.
Does my file leave my computer?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser.
