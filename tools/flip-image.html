Frequently Asked Questions

What does flipping an image do?

Flipping creates a mirror image. Horizontal flip mirrors left-to-right, vertical flip mirrors top-to-bottom. You can also combine both.

Can I preview before downloading?

Yes. The live preview updates instantly when you toggle flip options.

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WebP files are supported. All processing is done locally in your browser.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All flipping is performed entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
