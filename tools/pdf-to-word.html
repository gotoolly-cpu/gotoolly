Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PDF to Word converter free?
Yes, the PDF to Word converter is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, no registration required, and no usage limits.
Is it safe to convert PDF to Word here?
Yes. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server, and no data leaves your device.
Does my file leave my computer?
No. The entire conversion process happens client-side in your browser using JavaScript. No data is transmitted to any server.
Can I convert scanned PDFs to Word?
Scanned PDFs contain images of text, not actual text data. This converter works with text-based PDFs (created digitally from Word, Google Docs, etc.). Scanned documents require OCR technology which is not included.
Why doesn't the formatting always match?
PDF and Word use fundamentally different document models. PDF stores text at precise coordinates while Word uses flowing text. Complex layouts like multi-column designs and tables may not convert perfectly. Use Best Formatting mode for improved results.
Can I edit the generated DOCX file?
Yes. The generated DOCX file can be opened and edited in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, LibreOffice, or any other word processor that supports the DOCX format.
