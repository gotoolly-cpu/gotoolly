Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PowerPoint to PDF converter free?
Yes, it is completely free to use. No hidden fees, no registration required, and no usage limits.
Is it safe to convert PowerPoint to PDF?
Yes. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
Will animations and transitions be preserved?
No, slides are rendered as static images. Animations, transitions, and embedded media are not preserved in the PDF.
Can I convert .ppt files?
Only .pptx format is supported. The older .ppt format uses a binary format that cannot be parsed in the browser.
