    Frequently Asked Questions

    How is password strength calculated?

    The tool calculates Shannon entropy based on character diversity and length. It also checks against a list of common passwords, analyzes character types, and estimates crack time.

    What is entropy in password strength?

    Entropy measures the unpredictability of a password. Higher entropy means more possible combinations and stronger protection against brute-force attacks.

    Is my password uploaded to a server?

    No. All password analysis happens entirely in your browser. Your password never leaves your device.

    What makes a password strong?

    A strong password is at least 12-16 characters long, includes uppercase, lowercase, digits, and symbols, avoids dictionary words and common patterns, and has high entropy.
