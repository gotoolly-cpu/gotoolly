Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert PNG to JPG?

Upload your PNG images, adjust quality and background color, then click Convert. Each image is processed in your browser and available for download.

Will I lose transparency when converting PNG to JPG?

Yes. JPEG format does not support transparency. Any transparent areas in your PNG will be filled with the background color you select (white by default).

What quality setting should I use?

For photos, 85-95% offers excellent quality with good compression. For screenshots or text, 95-100% preserves sharpness.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All conversion happens entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

Can I convert multiple PNG files at once?

Yes. Select multiple PNG files and they will all be converted. You can download them individually or use the "Download All" button to get them as a ZIP file (requires JSZip library).
